Account & Data Deletion Request

You can delete your account directly inside the app, or submit a request by email and we will handle it for you within 7 days.

1
Option A – In-app (instant):
Open SportsConnect → Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.
2
Option B – Email request:
Send us an email from the address registered to your account.

Send your deletion request to

ansh1jan2005@gmail.com
Suggested subject: Account Deletion Request – [your registered email]
Include: your registered email address or username so we can locate your account.
What gets deleted: your profile, gigs you created, participation records, chat messages, and all other data tied to your account. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
